Discipleship Path SERVING Next Steps

With respect to SERVING, below are considerations to enhance your spiritual walk in Christ.

Exploring Christ

  • Explore your spiritual gifts by taking the spiritual gift assessment. [Spiritual Gifts Assessment]
  • [1 Corinthians 12:1-11]

Growing In Christ

  • Pick your top 3 dominant gifts.
  • Evaluate the needs and SERVING opportunities at the church.
  • Pray about which area(s) you want to get more involved in at the church.
  • [Romans 12:4-8]

Tip: Don't limit your gifts or SERVING opportunities to what shows up on the spiritual gift assessment, just use it as a starting point/guide.

Tip: Don't doubt your gifts.

Close To Christ

  • Use your gifts by SERVING in different areas in this church.
  • [1 Peter 4:10-11]
  • [James 2:14-26]

Christ-Centered

  • Start SERVING on a regular basis at the church.
  • Encourage others to SERVE as well.
  • [Ephesians 2:8-10]

Tip: Don't over commit.

Tip: Always remember the reason why you're SERVING is to bring honor and glory to God and to be the hands and feet of Jesus.

Tip: Remember we are only saved by grace alone, not by works.
