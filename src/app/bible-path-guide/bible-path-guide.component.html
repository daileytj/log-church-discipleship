Discipleship Path BIBLE STUDY Next Steps

With respect to BIBLE STUDY, below are considerations to enhance your spiritual walk in Christ. Jesus answered, “It is written: 'Man shall not live on bread alone, but on every word that comes from the mouth of God.' [Matt 4:4]

Exploring Christ

  • Choose a BIBLE that you can read easily and understand. A 'study Bible' is a good idea, as there is helpful information throughout. The best BIBLE is the one you read!
  • Here is a good guide to translations: Ultimate Guide To Bible Translations
  • Begin by reading The Gospels [Matthew, Mark, Luke & John] John is an excellent 1st book to read.
  • Consider a BIBLE app for your phone - YouVersion is an excellent one. This has BIBLE reading guides for beginners, as well as topical BIBLE studies

Growing In Christ

  • Read the BIBLE every day. Remember: you don't need to fully understand everything right away. The important thing is that you are feasting on God's Word!
  • As you read, pray about what you are reading, ask God to help you understand & how to apply to your life.
  • Begin to use a BIBLE STUDY guide, [e.g. on the YouVersion app] or a book of your choosing, to help you discover meaning in what you are reading. Ask your Pastor or a mentor for their recommendation.
  • Consider joining a small group BIBLE STUDY to begin to discuss Scripture with others. Ask questions!
  • As you read and study God's word regularly, you will discover that it will begin to be the authority in your life.
  • 2 Timothy 3:16-17

Close To Christ

  • Continue to study the BIBLE daily. A great way to study the Scripture is inductive study. Here is a website that explains this: Precept
  • Pray and reflect on what you are reading, let it shape the way you see everything in your life. Never be afraid to ask questions!
  • Consider a “read through the Bible” reading plan, which guides you through the entire BIBLE in a certain period of time - 6 months, 1 year, etc.
  • Continue BIBLE STUDY w/others.
  • Begin to memorize verses
  • The God's Word is becoming the authority in all that you do, guiding your decisions.
  • Joshua 1:8

Christ-Centered

  • The BIBLE, the Word of God, is now the guiding authority in your life as you read and reflect on it every day.
  • Continue to pray as you read, ask God to show you what He wants you to know
  • Apply what you read & learn for everyday life, use Biblical wisdom in all you do & say.
  • Continue to study with others, consider how you can share your knowledge of the BIBLE
  • Continue to memorize scripture
  • Remember - even though you have read a book, chapter, passage before, God can always reveal something new to you.
  • Psalm 119:5-7
