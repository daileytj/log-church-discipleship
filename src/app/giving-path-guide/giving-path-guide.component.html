Discipleship Path GIVING Next Steps

With respect to GIVING, below are considerations to enhance your spiritual walk in Christ. Of all the pillars in a christians spiritual maturity, GIVING is usually the last pillar to develop as God intended.

Exploring Christ

  • You have many reasons to suppress thoughts on GIVING [Matthew 6:21]

Growing In Christ

  • You do not give with an expectation of obtaining a blessing or reward. [Matthew 6:2]
  • You start to consider GIVING more seriously, as you become attune to the blessings God has given you. [Deuteronomy 16:17]

Close To Christ

  • GIVING of yourself is a crucial consideration [2 Corinthians 8:5]
  • You realize that GIVING is a part of your spiritual walk. [Matthew 6:21]
  • You GIVE from the top, not from your leftovers [Proverbs 3:9]

Christ-Centered

  • You prayerfully plan your GIVING each year
  • You contemplate sacrificial GIVING. [Exodus 36:5]
  • Everything belongs to God. [1 Chronicles 29:14]
