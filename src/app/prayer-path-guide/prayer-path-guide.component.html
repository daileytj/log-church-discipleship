With respect to PRAYING, below are considerations to enhance your spiritual walk in Christ. "But Jesus Himself would often slip away to the wilderness to pray" Luke 5:16

Exploring Christ

What

PRAYER is a conversation with God. It is words spoken from your heart to God's.

Where and When

Dedicate a daily time and place to PRAY.

How to Pray

How to Pray

Why Pray

To grow closer to God.

Expect to meet the Lord in PRAYER!

Growing In Christ

  • Choose a mentor or accountability partner to keep your promise of PRAYER
  • Start a PRAYER journal to document all the ways God is moving. Be certain to include PRAYERS of gratitude.
  • Set a goal or have a vision to measure spiritual progress.
  • PRAY in the Spirit [Jude 1:20]

Close To Christ

  • PRAY with others [Matthew 18:20]
  • PRAY for others [James 5:16]
  • PRAY bold PRAYERS and PRAY with expectancy!

Christ-Centered

  • PRAY with fasting as a means of denying the flesh by feeding only the spirit.
  • Minister to others through PRAYER. Examples include rebuking illness, breaking strongholds, evangelizing to the lost.
  • Listen for the nudging/voice of the Holy Spirit during PRAYER. Trust it by taking obedient action.
